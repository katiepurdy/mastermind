How to play:

The top row contains a secret combination of colored dots for you to try to recreate.

Guess the secret code in 10 or fewer guesses!

Each of the secret dots have been randomly chosen from the six possible colors to the left of these rules.

A colour can be used more than once, so the combination could contain anything from 4 different colors to 4 of the same color.

Once you've assigned all four dots a colour, click the "Test Your Guess" button.

Each time you test your guess, score pegs will appear in the square to the right of your guess to let you know how close that guess is to being correct.

Black pegs: Indicate that one of your dots is the correct color and also in the correct position.
Grey pegs: Indicate that one of your pegs is the correct color but is in the wrong position.

Use the pegs to guide your next guess. If your guess scores 4 black score pegs within 10 tries, you win.
